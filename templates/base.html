Help & Guide

How to use Money Mate

Dashboard

Your financial overview for the current month. See total income, spending, net balance, and daily burn rate. The dashboard auto-resets at the start of each new month — only subscriptions and recurring salary are kept.

Adding Income

Go to Income in the nav. Choose the source (Salary, Business, Freelance, etc.), enter the amount, and optionally pick the date it was received.

Check "Recurring / Monthly Salary?" so it's preserved when the dashboard resets each month.
Tracking Expenses

Go to Expenses and pick a main category and sub-category. Each expense is automatically tagged as a Need or Want based on the 50/30/20 rule.

Check "Recurring Subscription?" to set a start & end date and get expiry reminders.
Subscriptions

Automatically detects recurring expenses from the last 3 months. You can also manually mark any expense as a subscription. Set an end date to get a reminder 7 days before it expires.

Expired subscriptions show a Expired badge — tap the trash icon to remove them.
Savings Goals

Create goals with a target amount and monthly savings plan. The app calculates how many months it will take to reach your goal and shows a timeline with milestones.

Use the What-If tool on a goal to see how reducing spending can get you there faster.
The 50/30/20 Rule

A simple budgeting guideline:

  • 50% Needs — rent, food, utilities, healthcare
  • 30% Wants — dining out, entertainment, travel
  • 20% Savings — goals, investments, emergency fund

Money Mate automatically classifies your expenses and shows how close you are to each target.

Dark Mode

Click the moon/sun icon in the navbar to toggle dark mode. Your preference is saved automatically.
